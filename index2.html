Introduction

Electronics is the branch of physics that deals with the control and manipulation of electrical energy using electronic devices and circuits. It is a foundation of modern technology and has revolutionized almost every aspect of our lives, from communication and entertainment to transportation and healthcare. The study of electronics began with the discovery of the electron and has since evolved through the invention of the vacuum tube, transistor, and integrated circuit. Today, electronics is used in almost every industry and is an essential part of our daily lives. Understanding the principles of electronic devices and circuits is crucial for basic electronics engineering, which is an important foundation for more advanced fields such as digital electronics, power electronics,and communication systems.
